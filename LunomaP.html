About Us

Located in the heart of Tampa, Florida, Lunoma Painting has been transforming spaces across the Tampa Bay area since 2019. Our team of skilled artisans brings years of expertise to every project, whether it's a cozy home or a sprawling commercial complex.

We take pride in our commitment to excellence, using only premium-quality paints and materials to ensure durable, stunning finishes that stand the test of time. Our attention to detail and dedication to customer satisfaction have earned us the trust of numerous homeowners, businesses, and institutions throughout Central Florida.

From historic restorations in Ybor City to modern office spaces in downtown Tampa, our versatility and professionalism have made us a go-to painting service for clients who demand the best. We're not just painters; we're partners in bringing your vision to life, one brushstroke at a time.
